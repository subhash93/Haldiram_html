Privacy Policy

Haldiram India Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you engage with us, particularly regarding franchise inquiries, applications, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with us through our website, email, or franchise registration forms, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, contact number, email address, postal address, nationality, and identity proofs.
  • Business/Franchise Details: Proposed site location, property ownership documents (owned/rented/leased), lease agreements, investment details, and business preferences.
  • Eligibility Documents: Educational qualifications, proof of citizenship/residency (for Indian citizens and NRIs).
  • Communication Data: Any correspondence, feedback, or queries you send to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To process and evaluate franchise applications.
  • To verify eligibility and required compliance documents.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application status, franchise opportunities, and updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations, regulatory requirements, and government directives.
  • To improve our services, marketing, and operational processes.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information:

  • With internal teams and authorized representatives for processing franchise applications.
  • With legal and regulatory authorities, when required by law.
  • With third-party service providers (e.g., auditors, IT support, marketing agencies) bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only as long as necessary for processing franchise applications, fulfilling contractual obligations, complying with legal requirements, and resolving disputes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable).

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@haldiramfranchiseindia.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website or communication may include links to third-party platforms (such as Zomato, Swiggy, etc. for delivery services). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such external websites.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our official website with a revised “Effective Date.”
