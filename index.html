Haldiram’s Franchise Information

Greetings From Haldiram India Private Limited!

Thank you for exploring and inquiring about the Haldiram Franchise. Based on the inquiry for our franchise, please find enclosed a brief proposed for your disposal. This overview will help you in analyzing the core strength of the company which in turn will be beneficial for a smoother profitable and organized business.

Site Location & Space Requirement:

  • You will have to provide an appropriate site location with required carpet area as per the company’s requirement.
  • Proposed site should be located at the prime location of your city.
  • There should be available additional parking space for the vehicles of customers & Delivery Boy.
  • There should not be any other Haldiram franchise outlet within radius of 2.5 km of your proposed site location.
  • There must not be any government objection on the proposed site offered by you
  • Proposed site offered by the Applicant should be owned / rented / leased.
  • In case of lease / rent, the applicant will have to submit the rent agreement paper to the company within 3 months after getting the LOI
  • The starting lease agreement time period should be for minimum 2 years with a condition that franchisee can extend the time period of lease agreement after two years.

Eligibility Criteria for Main Applicant

  • You will have to provide an appropriate site location with required carpet area as per the company’s requirement.

Types of Franchise

QSR (Quick Service Restaurant)

  • Minimum Space requirement for QSR is 1,000 - 2,000 sq. ft. (The franchise owner can provide preferred location for the restaurant is ground floor that makes movement convenient, however a top floor with terrace and an exotic view is also acceptable in certain locations. Prime locations in city centers, near educational institutions and shopping malls are also suitable for franchise owner by him)
  • The interior design for at least 50 customers with sitting facilities for same at a time as well as consisting of waiting lobby with a holding capacity of at least 30 customers.
  • The approx. investment will be ₹ 60-1 Cr.
  • A total of 10 staffs (1 managing staff, 2 professional well trained chefs including kitchen staff, 1 billing operator, 4 servers and 2 helper ).

Supporting Facilities and Benefits

  • The company will always be bound to contribute in the growth in terms of scalability and profitability of its franchise holders
  • All the staffs will be hired & trained by the company itself.
  • 50% of the staff salary will be borne by the company itself for the initial agreement period.
  • In case of Leased/Rented property, 50% of the rent will be borne by the company for the initial agreement period
  • The company will provide the Haldiram app software for online orders and also tie up with other online delivery partners like Zomato, swiggy etc. (online orders cover 20% of the total sales).
  • All the festival offers discount will be borne by company itself.
  • The company’s team will be helping the franchisee to choose the most appropriate site for this setup without any assistance charge
  • Nowadays, in the era of stiff competition, the company will always try to increase sells of the franchisee by advertising the outlet in near market and advertisement cost will be borne by the company itself. This advertisement may be in Television, News Paper, Holding in the city.
  • If franchisee has some advertisement suggestion, then they may suggest to the company

Note- As per the government rule GST will be applicable on each payment. The cost of nterior and exterior design may vary depend on the city type, area and construction building
